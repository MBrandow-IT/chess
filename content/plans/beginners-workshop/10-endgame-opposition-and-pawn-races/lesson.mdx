  <Slide title="What is opposition?">
    Opposition means kings face each other with one square between them. The
    side **not** to move usually has the advantage in that moment.

    This can decide whether a king breaks through to support promotion.

    <ChessBoard fen="8/8/8/3k4/8/3K4/4P3/8 w - - 0 1" />
  </Slide>

  <Slide title="Pawn race method">
    In races:

    1. Count moves to promotion.
    2. Include checks and forcing king moves.
    3. Compare whose pawn queens first and whether that result is decisive.

    Accurate counting beats guesswork.
  </Slide>
