  <Slide title="Fork basics">
    A fork attacks two or more targets at once. If the opponent can save only
    one, you win material.

    Common fork targets:

    - King + queen
    - King + rook
    - Queen + unprotected minor piece

    <ChessBoard fen="r3k2r/ppp2ppp/2n5/3N4/8/8/PPP2PPP/R3K2R w KQkq - 0 1" />
  </Slide>

  <Slide title="Defending against forks">
    - Keep valuable pieces on different colors and files
    - Watch for enemy knight jump squares near your king and queen
    - Castle and coordinate pieces to reduce loose targets
    - If needed, give up a small concession to avoid a bigger tactic
  </Slide>

  <Slide title="Puzzle lab + homework setup">

    - Knight forks
    - Queen double attacks
    - Defensive moves that avoid forks

    **Homework idea:** In your next game, mark every move where a fork was
    possible for either side.
